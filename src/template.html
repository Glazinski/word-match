Generally

Game starts on the board which has 16 fields and each field has its letter. Your job is to find as many words as possible with these letters. Word has to be longer than three characters. You can't enter the same word twice.

Points

Points depend on length of the word the player has combined. The pattern looks like this: (Length of the word / 2) - 1) rounded up

Controls

Spacebar (use when game starts) - Shows keymap
